# -Kelly-Roland-Coffee-Shops-2023-Operational-Report
Project: Kelly Roland Coffee Shops - 2023 Operational Report Objective: To analyze the 2023 operational performance of Kelly Roland Coffee Shops by identifying key sales trends, peak performance periods, and product profitability to provide actionable insights for strategic decision-making.
Data & Tools: Utilized a transactional sales dataset sourced from Kaggle. The entire analysis, including data cleaning, transformation, and visualization, was performed using Microsoft Excel. Key features included PivotTables, advanced formulas (XLOOKUP, SUMIFS), and interactive dashboards.

Key Analysis & Insights:

Sales Performance: Identified a steady month-over-month growth in revenue, with Q4 (particularly December) being the strongest quarter, driven by holiday promotions.

Product Analysis: Determined the top-selling item by revenue and volume, while highlighting underperforming products for potential menu optimization.

Customer & Timing Trends: Peak sales hours were between 7-9 AM and 12-2 PM. Weekends showed higher average transaction values compared to weekdays.

Store Performance: Compared sales across different locations, pinpointing the highest and lowest-performing stores to understand regional variances.

Conclusion & Value: The analysis provided a clear, data-driven overview of the business's 2023 performance. The final interactive Excel dashboard offers management a single source of truth to track KPIs, optimize inventory, plan staffing for peak hours, and tailor marketing strategies to boost profitability in 2024.
